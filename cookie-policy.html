Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization
  • Marketing Cookies: These are used to deliver relevant advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 First-Party Cookies

These are cookies set directly by our website and are only readable by us.

3.2 Third-Party Cookies

These are cookies set by third-party services we use, such as Google Analytics.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Through your browser settings - most browsers allow you to refuse cookies
  • Through third-party tools and opt-out mechanisms
  • By contacting us directly

5. US-Specific Information

For users in the United States:

  • We comply with applicable US privacy laws regarding cookie usage
  • California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA
  • You can opt-out of certain tracking through industry opt-out tools
