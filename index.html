About YETINSA

YETINSA is a nationally recognized high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, manufacturing, and sales of RGB LED components, LED display unit and LED strips.

Leveraging its profound expertise in the optoelectronic industry, the company integrates dedicated teams for research and development, business operations, and marketing. Committed to meeting customer demands, it aspires to be a global leader in the small pitch display sector.

Our Capabilities

The company's technological prowess and product portfolio span diverse fields. Drawing on its robust capabilities in the encapsulation of optoelectronic devices, it maintains a leading position domestically in various technical domains, including:

  • MLED micro-pitch display technology
  • Advanced LED lighting solutions
  • Large-screen display techniques
